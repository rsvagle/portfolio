VirtualScope

VirtualScope is a project conceived by a biology professor at Metropolitan State University needing to give her students access to their experiments remotely. The vision was to use a Raspberry Pi for streaming video and capturing images from a microscope and then presenting those on a website for students who arenâ€™t on campus. Raspberry Pis run Python code to query the database, open and maintain an ftp connections and switch between recording video and taking photos for the site. My (amazing) team and I learned and used PHP to dynamically present web pages and allow administrators to access student login records and configure the microscopes they're using.
